Our Services

Procedures We Coordinate

Access world-class cosmetic surgery at Shanghai's leading clinics, with full concierge support from first enquiry through to full recovery.

FUE Hair Transplant

The gold standard in hair restoration — minimally invasive, no linear scarring, natural density that lasts a lifetime.

Ideal for: Men & women with thinning or receding hairlines. Results from 6–12 months.

Porcelain Veneers

Ultra-thin porcelain shells to correct discoloration, gaps, chips and misalignment — a Hollywood smile, instantly.

Ideal for: Anyone wanting a radiant, flawless smile. Results immediate, lasting 10–15+ years.

Rhinoplasty

Precision nose reshaping for harmony and balance. Both cosmetic refinement and structural correction supported.

Ideal for: Subtle refinement or structural correction. Final result at 6–12 months.

Eyelid Surgery

Blepharoplasty and double eyelid creation for a refreshed, wide-awake look — taking years off your appearance.

Ideal for: Double eyelid creation or anti-aging correction. Recovery in 1–2 weeks.

Breast Augmentation

Enhance shape, size, and symmetry using premium silicone implants or fat transfer, personalised to your body.

Ideal for: Women seeking fuller, proportionate contours. Final shape settles in 3–6 months.

Lip Enhancement

Hyaluronic acid fillers expertly sculpted for natural volume, definition, and the perfect pout — no overdone results.

Ideal for: Subtle plumping or fuller definition. Natural results visible in 3–4 weeks as swelling settles. Minimal downtime.
