About This Procedure

What is Rhinoplasty?

Rhinoplasty (nose reshaping surgery) refines the nose to improve its appearance and proportion in relation to the face. It can address the bridge, tip, nostrils, and overall profile — creating a more balanced, harmonious result.

Shanghai's rhinoplasty surgeons are highly experienced in both open and closed techniques, with a particular specialty in natural-looking results that complement East and Southeast Asian facial features. The high volume of procedures performed means surgeons develop a precision and consistency that is difficult to match elsewhere.

Vday coordinates your full trip — surgeon consultation, pre-operative assessments, clinic visits, accommodation, and aftercare check-ins — so your focus stays entirely on your results.

What to Expect

The Procedure Process

Your surgeon will assess your nasal anatomy and facial proportions during the consultation, then recommend an approach — open or closed rhinoplasty — based on your goals. Surgery is performed under general anaesthesia.

A nasal splint is worn for 7–10 days post-surgery. Most visible swelling and bruising resolves within 2–4 weeks. You will look presentable to most people within 2 weeks. Final results, as residual swelling fully settles, emerge at 6–12 months.

Common Questions

Rhinoplasty FAQ

Is rhinoplasty permanent?
Yes. The structural changes made during rhinoplasty are permanent. The nose may continue to change subtly with age, but the surgical result is lasting.
Does rhinoplasty hurt?
The procedure is performed under general anaesthesia — you are fully asleep and feel nothing. Post-operative discomfort is manageable with prescribed medication. Most clients describe it as pressure rather than pain.
What is the difference between open and closed rhinoplasty?
Open rhinoplasty involves a small incision between the nostrils, giving the surgeon greater access to reshape the structure. Closed rhinoplasty uses internal incisions only, leaving no visible external scarring. Your surgeon will recommend the best approach based on your anatomy and goals.
When will swelling go down after rhinoplasty?
Most visible swelling and bruising resolves within 2–4 weeks. Final results, as residual swelling fully settles, emerge at 6–12 months.
