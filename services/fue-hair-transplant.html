What is FUE Hair Transplant?

Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE) is the most advanced hair restoration technique available today. Individual hair follicles are extracted one at a time from the donor area — usually the back and sides of the scalp — and precisely implanted into thinning or balding areas. There is no linear scar and minimal downtime, which is why FUE has become the preferred method over older strip-harvesting techniques.

Shanghai's leading clinics perform FUE at exceptional volume, giving their surgical teams a level of precision and speed that's difficult to match elsewhere. Vday coordinates your entire trip — consultation, procedure, accommodation, and aftercare — so you can focus on recovery, not logistics.

The Procedure Process

Your surgeon will assess your donor density, hair loss pattern, and goals during consultation, then map out a hairline and graft plan tailored to your face. The procedure itself is performed under local anaesthesia and typically takes 6–10 hours depending on graft count, with a short break partway through.

Initial shedding of the transplanted hairs occurs at 2–6 weeks — this is completely normal and expected. New growth begins around 3 months, with significant density visible by 6 months and full results settling in at 9–12 months.
