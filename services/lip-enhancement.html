What is Lip Enhancement?

Lip enhancement uses dermal filler — typically hyaluronic acid — to add volume, define the lip border, improve symmetry, and create a more balanced facial profile. It is a non-surgical treatment with results visible immediately and minimal recovery required.

Hyaluronic acid fillers are fully reversible and dissolvable, making lip enhancement one of the most flexible, low-commitment aesthetic treatments available — ideal if you want to start subtle and build up over time.

The Procedure Process

A topical numbing cream is applied before treatment. Your doctor will discuss your goals and recommend the appropriate filler volume and placement to complement your natural anatomy. The procedure itself takes 30–60 minutes.

Some swelling and minor bruising is normal for 2–3 days afterward. Final results are visible within a week once swelling fully settles, and typically last 6–12 months depending on the product used and your individual metabolism.
